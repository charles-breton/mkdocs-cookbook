# INGREDIENTS

(Exemple for 1 lbs of fish)
  

2 cups gluten free cereal flakes

1/3 cup roasted pumpkin seeds

zest of 1 lemon

1 tsp garlic powder

1 tsp paprika

1/3 cup brown rice flour

2 large organic egg whites
