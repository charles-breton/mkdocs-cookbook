# INGREDIENTS

- [ ] 2 pounds Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
- [ ] 1 cup pecans, roughly chopped
- [ ] 2 tablespoons olive oil
- [ ] 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
- [ ] Kosher salt and black pepper

---
# INSTRUCTIONS

1. Heat oven to 400Â° F. On a large rimmed baking sheet, toss Brussels sprouts, pecans, oil, garlic, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper. Turn the Brussels sprouts cut-side down.
    
2. Roast for about 20 to 25 minutes, or until the Brussels sprouts are golden and tender.
