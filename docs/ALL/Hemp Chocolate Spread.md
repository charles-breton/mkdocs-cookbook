# INGREDIENTS

- [ ] 1/4 cup hemp seeds
- [ ] 1/3 cup raw cacao
- [ ] 1/4 cup ghee or coconut oil
- [ ] 2 tbsp honey
- [ ] Pinch of sea salt

---
# INSTRUCTIONS

1. Process all ingredients together until smooth. Add more sweetener as desired to taste.
